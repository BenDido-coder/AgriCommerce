Help Center

Frequently Asked Questions

What is AgriCommerce and how does it work?

AgriCommerce is an Ethiopian online agricultural marketplace that connects farmers, suppliers, and buyers. Sellers can list their products, and buyers can browse, contact them via WhatsApp, and arrange delivery and payments directly.

Who can use this platform?

Anyone in Ethiopia—farmers, wholesalers, or buyers—can use the platform. You only need a phone number (preferably with WhatsApp) to start.

How do I list my agricultural products for sale?

Create an account, go to your dashboard, click “Post a Product”, fill in the details, and upload images. Your post will be reviewed and published soon after.

How can I contact a seller or buyer?

Click the “Contact via WhatsApp” button on any listing to directly message the user. You can also call them if a number is provided.

Is payment handled through the website?

No. Payments are made directly between the buyer and seller using options like telebirr, HelloCash, or CBE Birr. Confirm all details before paying.

How do I know a product or seller is trustworthy?

We verify phone numbers and review listings. Check reviews, ask for recent photos, and confirm details before payment. Trust your instincts.

Can I buy products from a different region?

Yes! The site works across all Ethiopian regions. Delivery must be arranged between the buyer and seller using local transportation options.

Is there a fee to use the website?

No, it’s currently free to use. In the future, we may add optional paid features like promoted listings.

What types of products are allowed?

We allow crops, livestock, tools, seeds, fertilizers, and dried foods. Illegal or expired items are not allowed and will be removed.

What should I do if I face a problem with a trade?

Use the “Report” button or contact support via WhatsApp. We’ll review the issue and take action if necessary.
