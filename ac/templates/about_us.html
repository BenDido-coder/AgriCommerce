About AgriCommerce

Connecting Ethiopian farmers directly to buyers through digital innovation

Our Journey

Empowering Agricultural Trade

AgriCommerce emerged from Gelan Boys' Boarding Secondary School's IT Department as a solution to Ethiopia's agricultural challenges. Our platform bridges the gap between small-scale farmers and national markets, eliminating exploitative middlemen through digital innovation.

Key features driving change:

  • Direct farmer-to-buyer transactions
  • Real-time market price updates
  • Integrated logistics support
  • Secure digital payments (M-Pesa integration)
  • Dual-language support (Amharic & English)

Our Mission

Modernize Ethiopia's agricultural sector through digital marketplace solutions that ensure fair pricing and market access.

Our Vision

To become Ethiopia's premier agricultural trading platform, serving 1 million farmers by 2030.
